Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need location permission?

The app uses your device's GPS to calculate the sun's exact position, direction, and angle based on where you are. Without location access, the core functionality cannot work.

How do I enable location access?

Go to your device Settings → Sun Tracker → Location and select "While Using the App" or "Always".

The sun position seems inaccurate. What should I do?

Make sure location permission is set to precise (not approximate), and that you have a clear GPS signal outdoors. If the issue persists, please contact us.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at vincentma.code@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version.
